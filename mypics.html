Food/Water


Unfortunately, no luxury meals in the woods. You have to hunt. Ofcourse, gathering plants and nuts are an option, but that wont keep you energetic for long. So, naturally, hunting is your best option. It woul help if you have a knife. If you dont, then no worries. Ok. So first off, if you do have a knife, then you're in luck. You can now make spears, traps, fires, bows, arrows, fishing rods, and so many other things. Or, if you have talent with knife throwing, there you go. The key point is to hunt to keep yourself alive. Remember to always clean the animals properly, and make sure they dont have diseases.
